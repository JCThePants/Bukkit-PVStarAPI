Location respawn ( )

Respawn the specified player at a spawnpoint appropriate to their current arena relation.

i.e. a lobby player gets teleported to one of the lobby spawns.

This does not force a dead player to respawn. It simply teleports the player back to once of the spawnpoints related to the players current arena context relation.

Returns
The location the player was spawned at or null if failed.
