Detailed Description

Called after a player is removed from an arena.

Not to be confused with PlayerPreRemoveFromContextEvent and PlayerRemovedFromContextEvent, which are used when a player is removed from an arenas IContextManager. (ie. lobby, game or spectator)

Although PlayerPreRemoveFromContextEvent and PlayerRemovedFromContextEvent are called before PlayerLeaveArenaEvent, in cases where the player is not actually leaving the arena (RemoveFromContextReason#CONTEXT_CHANGE), PlayerLeaveArenaEvent is not called.
